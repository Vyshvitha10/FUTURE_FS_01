Our story

Started in one kitchen, with one oven.

Sunrise Bakehouse began in 2018 when Meera started baking sourdough for friends and neighbours out of her home kitchen. Word got around the street, the orders outgrew the kitchen, and in 2019 we opened our doors on Subedari Road.

"We still mix every dough by hand, in the quantities we'd want to eat ourselves."
— Meera Reddy, Founder & Head Baker
