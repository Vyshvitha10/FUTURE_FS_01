Who I am

I'm a B.Tech Computer Science student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore, with a strong foundation in Java, Python, and web development. Over the past few years I've shipped 6+ hands-on projects spanning game development, data structures, and cross-platform desktop applications.
